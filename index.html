Special Dish

Jajangmyon

Jajangmyeon is a Korean-Chinese dish featuring thick wheat noodles topped with a savory black bean sauce made from chunjang, diced pork or beef, and vegetables. It's known for its rich, umami flavor and is often garnished with cucumber or pickled radish.

Special Selection

Happy Hour

  • So-tteok

    Seasonal $5.99

    Sotteok (소떡소떡) is a Korean street food skewer with chewy rice cakes and savory sausages, coated in a sweet and spicy gochujang sauce. It's a popular snack for its delicious mix of flavors and textures.

  • Corn Cheese

    $5.99

    Corn cheese features sweet corn mixed with mayonnaise and melted cheese, often served sizzling as a creamy, savory side dish or appetizer.

  • Kimchi Pancake

    $12.49

    Kimchi pancake, or kimchi jeon, is a crispy Korean dish made from a batter mixed with chopped kimchi, offering a tangy, spicy flavor. It's enjoyed as a snack or side dish.

  • Fried Chicken Wings

    New $9.99

    Korean fried chicken wings are crispy, double-fried wings coated in a flavorful glaze, often sweet, spicy, or soy-based.

  • Tofu Stew

    $12.49

    Tofu stew, or **soondubu jjigae** in Korean, is a hearty, spicy dish made with soft tofu, vegetables, and often seafood or meat. It's simmered in a flavorful broth seasoned with gochujang (Korean chili paste) or gochugaru (chili flakes) and typically served bubbling hot.

  • Pork Belly

    $14.49

    Pork belly is a rich, tender cut with layers of fat, popular for grilling or slow-cooking in dishes like Korean samgyeopsal. Its versatility and flavor make it a favorite in various cuisines.
